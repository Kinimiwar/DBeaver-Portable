Setup JAVA:
1) Download the zipped JRE (x86,x64,both depends on your variant of portable): "http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/java/javase/downloads/index.html"
2) Extract the files in App\java_xx

N.B:
The zipped JRE contains just one folder: jre_XXX_YYY
XXX = version
YYY = update
You need the extract the files that are inside that folder:
e.g
Correct folder layout:
App\java_xx\bin\javaw.exe
